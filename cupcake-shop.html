About Us

The three of us came from different backgrounds but we all had an interest to learn coding. We met at Epicodus and one of the practices in the lesson wanted us to create a cupcake shop. Alex came up with the birthday cake idea, Frank came up with the red velvet idea, and Jonathan came up with the matcha idea. After graduating from Epicodus, we decided to implement our ideas from this lesson and open a cupcake shop while we look for jobs in the tech industry. Hopefully, our cupcakes can sweeten up your lives just as they do to ours.
